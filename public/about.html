About Plink

Plink is a score tracking app for game nights, recurring card matches, and casual points-based competitions. The goal is simple: make it easy to start a session quickly, keep the scoreboard readable during play, and preserve useful history after the game ends.

What The App Covers

You can create sessions for players or teams, save common setups, follow score changes as they happen, keep timers moving, and review performance trends after the match. The app is built for repeated use, not only for one-off score entry.

Guest Mode And Accounts

Plink supports guest mode for quick local use on one device. If you want persistent history across devices, you can sign in and sync your saved sessions, players, and statistics.

Who It Is For

The app is useful for households, friend groups, and small communities that regularly track points across rounds and want more structure than handwritten notes or generic counters.
