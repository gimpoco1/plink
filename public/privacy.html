Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Plink helps you track game sessions, players, scores, and stats. This policy explains what data the app stores and how account sync works.

Data You Create

The app stores sessions, player names, avatar colors, score history, timer settings, and related gameplay stats.

Guest Mode

If you are not signed in, your data is saved on the device/browser you are using. It is not available on other devices unless you sign in and add it to your account or export a backup file.

Account Sync

If you sign in, Plink uses Supabase authentication and database storage to sync your account data. Your email address is used for authentication. Your sessions and players are stored so they can be restored across devices.

Backup Files

Backup files are created only when you choose to download them. They may contain your sessions, players, and score history. You control where those files are saved and who can access them.

Third-Party Services

Plink uses Supabase for authentication and cloud sync when account features are enabled. Supabase may process authentication and database data needed to provide those features. The Plink website may use Google AdSense for advertising. The iOS app does not load AdSense. Stripe processes website subscription payments; payment card details are handled by Stripe and are not stored by Plink.

Delete Your Account

Signed-in users can permanently delete their account from the account dialog in Plink. This removes the account and associated cloud data and cancels an active Stripe subscription. You can also request help at support@plinkscore.com.

Contact

For privacy or support requests, use the support page: /support.html.
