Plink Support

Need help with sessions, player profiles, account sync, backup files, or restoring data? Email support@plinkscore.com and include any details that can help reproduce the issue.

Before Contacting Support

  • Confirm whether you are signed in or using guest mode.
  • Check whether the missing data is saved on another device.
  • If you have a backup file, keep it available for restore help.
